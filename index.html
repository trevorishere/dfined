Services & Pricing

01

Microblading

Hair-stroke technique using a fine blade to create individual, natural-looking brow strokes. Ideal for sparse brows or those wanting a realistic, defined shape that mimics real hair.

Starting at $100

02

Powder / Ombré Brows

A soft, powdered finish that fades from lighter at the front to deeper at the tail — mimicking the look of filled brows. Perfect for oily skin types or those who love a polished, makeup-like finish.

Starting at $100

03

Nano Brows

Using a single digital needle for ultra-fine, precise hair strokes. Nano brows offer greater detail and longevity than traditional microblading — suitable for nearly all skin types.

Starting at $100

04

Lip Blushing

A semi-permanent tint that enhances your lip color, definition, and symmetry. Wake up every morning with the perfect natural flush — no lipstick required.

Starting at $100
